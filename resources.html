Using The Map

  1. The + and - symbols on the bottom left corner of the map can be used to zoom in or out of the map.
  2. Press and hold left click on your mouse or touchpad and move in your desired direction.
  3. Each blue pin represents a resource in the community. Click the pin to see the resource information.
  4. If you would prefer not to scroll, consider pressing the navigation menu button on the top left corner of the map to see each resource listed and filter by category.

Note: On smaller displays such as tablet or mobile devices, press the view larger map button on the top right corner of the map.
